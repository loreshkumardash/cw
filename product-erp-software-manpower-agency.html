What This Product Offers

Candidate Database Management

An ERP Software for Manpower agencies facilitates the efficient management of candidate profiles, including resumes, skills, qualifications, and work history. It enables easy searching, sorting, and categorizing of candidates based on various parameters.

Job Requisition Portal

Such software allows agencies to post job openings across multiple platforms, websites, and social media channels. It helps manage the entire job lifecycle, from requisition to candidate placement.

Resume Parsing & Matching

Advanced algorithms and AI-driven tools within the ERP software match candidate profiles with job requirements automatically and reduce the manual effort for finding suitable matches.

Timesheet and Payroll Management

ERP systems assist in tracking employee work hours, managing timesheets, calculating wages, and streamlining payroll processes. It ensures accuracy in payments and helps in managing financial aspects efficiently.

Training & Skill Development

Some ERP systems incorporate modules for managing training programs, skill enhancement, and certifications for candidates, ensuring a pool of skilled and updated talent.

Mobile Accessibility

With the growing need for mobility, having mobile-compatible ERP software allows recruiters to access critical information on the go, manage tasks, communicate with candidates, and respond promptly to client requests.

Client Management Portal

This feature enables agencies to maintain Relationships with clients by managing client information, preferences, contracts, and communications. It understands client needs and provides suitable candidate recommendations.

Candidate Self-Service Portal

These systems provide comprehensive reporting and analytics tools that offer insights into key performance indicators (KPIs), such as candidate placements, client satisfaction, revenue generated, and more.

Document Management System

Compliance Reporting

The Manpower agencies need various labour laws, regulations, and compliance standards. Our ERP software ensures that all processes and placements comply with legal requirements, minimizing risks.
